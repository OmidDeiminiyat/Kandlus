Admiral Richard E Byrd

Admiral Richard E. Byrd was the first to fly over the North and South Poles. After exploring the Arctic, he explored Antarctica. He then said in a television interview:

"But strangely enough, there is an area in the world today the size of the United States that has never been seen by humans, and that beyond the Pole and other side of South pole"

Finally, on December 24, 1956, after 10 years of exploring the Pole, he wrote a diary of a great mystery he had witnessed.
